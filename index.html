How we met



Believe it or not, Chace & Heather first met while out with friends in Downtown Denver at a little basement bar called Pat's. Heather was out with friends and Chace was too, after celebrating a big Alabama football win. They sat together and chatted for some time and one of Heather's friends managed to snap a picture of them. They went their separate ways and didn't keep in touch until one day many years later....

They re-connected via Facebook (it's good for something) when Chace friend requested the cute blonde to chat. Heather noticed the picture her friend had taken from that night years earlier and it was that same guy! They talked and text each other for over a month before Chace took her out on their first date. He picked her up where she lived in downtown Denver and Chace took her to see A Christmas Carol with a group of friends and their relationship grew from there.

Had they not connected on social media and Heather realized they had met before we might not be getting together to celebrate their love for each other on December, 14th.



Events

13th December

Explore The Broadmoor & Colorado Springs!

If you choose to travel to Colorado Springs the day before the event there is plenty of things to do and explore. We hope you can have some fun!

Welcome Party - Golden Bee (Optional) 8:30PM - ?

The Welcome Party will take place on Friday at the Golden Bee on the corner of Lake Avenue and First Street. If you head East from Lake Avenue's Main Building, you will cross Lake Circle. The Bee is located on the North side at the end of the block. The dress code at the Golden Bee is smart casual. This is optional if you come to town the day before, so stop in to say hi if you can!

14th December

Ceremony - Pauline Chapel 3:30PM to ~ 4:15PM

There will be a revolving shuttle service to transport all guests to the Pauline chapel on Saturday for the ceremony; similarly, after the ceremony this same service will return all guests to the Main Building to disembark for cocktail hour and reception. The shuttle service's pickup and drop-off location is at the valet area at the Main Building entrance, and the shuttle service will promptly begin at 2:30pm. Look for a shuttle with Gray Line Transportation on it.

Cocktail Hour 6PM - 7PM

Cocktail hour will be held in the festive red Pompeiian Room which is located on the East side Mezzanine Level of the Main Building. You can access the Mezzanine Level by taking the elevators or escalators next to the main lobby’s concierge desk.

Reception 7PM - 10PM

The reception will take place in the 1920’s French-era Main Ballroom that is Southern adjacent to the Pompeiian Room on the Mezzanine Level.

Dress Codes?

We request that all guests follow the Black-Tie Optional dress code for all events on Saturday. Guidelines can be found at Brides.com, GreenWeddingShoes, and Vogue.

While we are not asking guests to dress in certain colors, we do ask that guests avoid wearing white, silver, or any light colors that might look bridal. White button-up shirts under jackets are an exception.

Additionally, we want to give our guests a heads up that the bridesmaids will be wearing a dark green if you want to avoid that color.

What about small children?

Children are welcome to attend the welcome party, cocktail hour, and the reception, and we please ask that you register them and any dietary restrictions/allergies on the RSVP page.

Due to limited space in the chapel, children will not be able to attend the ceremony unless there is room.

The Broadmoor offers in-room nanny service if you are staying at the hotel, and a group nanny service will be provided to entertain any children during the ceremony. All information can be found at: broadmoornannies.com
